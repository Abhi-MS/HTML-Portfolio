Must watch Sci-fi movies

A low budget movie that would really make you think. This movie left me speechless by how good a movie can be just by its story line

Predestination

Have you ever thought, "if I could go back in time and fix this, the world would be a better place now"? Then this is the ultimate movie for you

Interstellar

My all time favourite movie. This is the definition of a great movie in every aspect.
